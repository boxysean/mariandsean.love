We are getting married!

Friday, September 21, 2018

Wedding Location

The wedding will be at Schloss Gamlitz in the south of Austria, nestled in the heart of the Austrian wine country, close to where Marianne is from.

Schloss Gamlitz is a 2.5 hour drive south of Vienna, and 30 minutes away from Graz.

Schedule

  • Thursday, September 20: Welcome

    In the evening, we'll welcome those of you who arrive early with some traditional Austrian music and food at a Buschenschank, a rustic family-run vineyard.

  • Friday, September 21: Wedding

    We'll get married in the afternoon, followed by a reception, dinner and a party in a Schloss Gamlitz's wine cellar! Just as a heads up: The evening will be for adults only, but we'll post information on babysitters here later.

  • Saturday, September 22: Brunch

    We'll bid you farewell with a brunch at Schloss Gamlitz.
