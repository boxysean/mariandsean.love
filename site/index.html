Wedding Location and Getting Around

The wedding will be at Schloss Gamlitz in the south of Austria, nestled in the heart of the Austrian wine country, close to where Marianne is from.

Schloss Gamlitz is a 2.5 hour drive south of Vienna, and 30 minutes away from Graz.

For train travelers, the closest train station to Gamlitz is Ehrenhausen, about 10 minutes away.

Uber and Lyft don't work in the region, so here are a couple taxi phone numbers:

  • Taxi Petra: +43 644 2070207
  • Taxi Schimautz: +43 664 4352736
